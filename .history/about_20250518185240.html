About Developer

My name is カソラ (Kasora). I am the solo developer of Skeb Sniper, a private commercial software, as well as the owner, developer, and manager of this website.

Since I live in the United States, I often missed the opportunity to commission artists on Skeb because of the time difference. In my case, I had been waiting for over two years to commission やふくろ (yafukuro), only to realize that their slots usually opened in the afternoon in Japan, which is early morning for me. By the time I was awake, their commissions were already closed. Since they only open two to three times a year, I never really had a chance to commission them, and I just couldn't compete with commissioners in the same time zone as the artist. I also learned that a friend of mine woke up at 3 am just to send a request to their favorite artists because their slots always fill up in a couple of minutes, so I created Skeb Sniper to solve this problem for myself and my friends who also struggle to get a slot from popular artists. Now with Skeb Sniper, whether we're sleeping, in a meeting, or traveling, we can always send a request to our favorite artists without disrupting whatever we're doing.

I came up with the name Skeb Sniper because a friend of mine said he had to "snipe" when he woke up at 3 am to send a request. Sniping feels like the right word because it’s all about grabbing your slot as fast as you can, but now we’re using a program to handle all that annoying stuff.

Finally, I would like to thank the amazing artists who made the drawings for me on this website. The icon and background were illustrated by とおぼえふうすけ (tooboefuusuke), and the portrait on the right was created by がみ (gami).
